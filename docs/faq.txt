2. Home Automation
What home automation solutions does SmartOhm offer?
Solutions include smart lighting, climate control, motorized curtains, AV automation, and centralized control systems for lights, appliances, and security.

Which smart lighting systems are available?
Wi-Fi and Zigbee-based smart bulbs, switches, and dimmers that integrate with voice assistants like Alexa, Google Home, and Siri.

Can I control my home remotely?
Yes, via mobile apps on iOS/Android. Control lights, AC, fans, curtains, and more from anywhere with internet access.

What voice control options are supported?
Full integration with Amazon Alexa, Google Assistant, and Apple HomeKit for hands-free operation.
